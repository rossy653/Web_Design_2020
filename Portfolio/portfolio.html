Rositsa Kostova

Nature, femininity and tradition are the cheered subjects in my creative artwork. I seek to combine old traditions with modern techniques and ideas. Capturing the beauty and elegance in even everyday objects reminds me that femininity is everywhere and it is so powerful. I use Illustration, painting, drawing and often mixed media to express the emotions and clarity in the simplest moments. My goal is to capture the little moments in life that seem so insignificant and show that they are more valuable and beautiful than we think.
